About This Project

What We Monitor

ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer - Real 501(c)(4) tax filings across all 50 states. FEC API - Campaign finance and PAC expenditures. Google News RSS - Real-time news. Craigslist - Job postings in major metros.

Why It Matters

Manufactured grassroots campaigns undermine democratic participation. When paid actors fill city council chambers or fake organizations flood legislators with letters, genuine public voices get drowned out.

Privacy First

We do not collect personal information. All analysis is performed on organizational data, hiring patterns, and financial disclosures - never on individuals or legitimate protesters.

Open Source

This entire project is open source. Review our methodology, audit our code, and contribute improvements. Transparency about our own operations is essential to our credibility.
